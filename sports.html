Cross Country

I ran cross country from 5th grade to senior year. I am now a head coach at a local middle school. Some of my favorite memories are being an alternate for a state winning team as a sophomore, placing ninth at districts as a senior, and the last week of practice as a coach.

Track

I participated in track as freshman, junior, and senior. Some of my favorite memories are running 2:10 for the first time, running the 4x400 relay, and junior year track season.

Basketball

I played basketball in middle school and also as junior and senior. I love playing pickup with friends and also joining 3on3 tournaments. I like watching the NBA. The Chicago Bulls are my favorite team in the league. A good memory I have relating to basketball are going to a Bulls vs Blazers game in Portland and seeing Zach Lavine score 33 points. Another good memory is dunking for the first time.

Knowledge Bowl

I did knowledge bowl as a sophomore and senior. Senior year we placed 2nd at state. My favorite memory is getting a question right using a dinosaur hotwheels car at the state meet.
